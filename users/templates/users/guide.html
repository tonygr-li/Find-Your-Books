You need to create an account only if you wish to publish a post (Want or Sell) or report a user. Buying a book and viewing posts does not require one.

This website uses contains search. This means that the website searches for posts that contains your search. For example, if you search for the ISBN “123”, it will look for all occurrences of “123” in our database. This means that results for this search would include “1234567891011”, “2341233447672”, and “3212394583582”, but it won’t detect “1, 2, 3” or “1-2-3”. This is why the most reliable way for searching is to use the ISBN of the book you wish to buy/sell, instead of the name or the author of the book.
