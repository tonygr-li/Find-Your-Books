About Us



Find Your Books was founded by student organizers of BrébeufHx, Collège Jean-de-Brébeuf’s annual hackathon. Our goal is to simplify the exchange of second hand school books to minimize waste generated by buying new ones. In addition, we encourage student programmers to build powerful applications by showing its feasibility and use in the real world to solve problems.

Want to learn more about coding? Visit www.brebeufhackathon.com to learn more about our annual hackathon event.

For those that are curious, this website uses the following programming languages:

Front-end: HTML/CSS with Bootstrap and Semantic-UI

Back-end: Django framework (Python)
